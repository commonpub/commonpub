About This Contest

IoT Mayhem is our annual celebration of creative hardware projects. This year's theme: MQTT madness. Build something that communicates over MQTT — the weirder, the better.

We're looking for projects that push boundaries, explore unconventional ideas, and demonstrate the maker spirit. Whether it's a smart plant that tweets or a distributed network of rubber ducks, we want to see your wildest IoT dreams come to life.
